## AWTEW-ACT3-SG02：Bertram's Impossible Letter

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT3-SG02
- 时长建议：10-15s
- 场景：Rousillon，COUNT's palace.
- 人物：COUNTESS, Clown, HELENA
- 内容：Letters reveal BERTRAM's rejection of HELENA. He declares he will not be her husband until she gets his ring and bears his child, conditions meant to be impossible.

## AWTEW-ACT3-SG03：Helena Chooses Exile

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT3-SG03
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Rousillon，COUNT's palace，private chamber.
- 人物：HELENA
- 内容：HELENA blames herself for driving BERTRAM into danger and decides to leave Rousillon as a pilgrim, hoping her absence will draw him safely home.

## AWTEW-ACT3-SG07：Helena Finds Diana

## AWTEW-ACT3-SG09：Bertram Boasts of Diana

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT3-SG09

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT3-SG10
- 时长建议：10-15s
- 场景：Florence，Widow's house.
