# All's Well That Ends Well — Act V Shot Groups

Source script: ../scripts/act-5.md

## AWTEW-ACT5-SG01：Helena Misses the King at Marseilles

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT5-SG01
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Marseilles，a street.
- 人物：HELENA, Widow, DIANA, Gentleman, attendants
- 内容：HELENA, DIANA, and the Widow arrive exhausted from travel and seek access to the KING. A Gentleman tells them the KING has already left for Rousillon.

## AWTEW-ACT5-SG02：The Petition Sent Ahead

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT5-SG02
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Marseilles，street outside lodgings.
- 人物：HELENA, Gentleman, Widow, DIANA
- 内容：HELENA asks the Gentleman to carry her petition to the KING and help bring DIANA before him. The group redirects the final proof toward Rousillon.

## AWTEW-ACT5-SG03：Parolles at the Gate

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT5-SG03
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Rousillon，before the COUNT's palace.
- 人物：PAROLLES, Clown
- 内容：PAROLLES, now poor and disgraced, follows the Clown and begs to have a letter delivered to LAFEU. The former braggart is reduced to dependence and ridicule.

## AWTEW-ACT5-SG04：Lafeu Takes in Parolles

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT5-SG04
- 时长建议：6-8s
- 场景：Rousillon，before the COUNT's palace.
- 人物：LAFEU, PAROLLES, Clown
- 内容：LAFEU recognizes PAROLLES's fall and, despite contempt for him, offers food and a place in service. PAROLLES accepts humility as his new condition.

## AWTEW-ACT5-SG05：The King Mourns Helena

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT5-SG05
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Rousillon，COUNT's palace，formal hall.
- 人物：KING, COUNTESS, LAFEU, First Lord, Second Lord, attendants
- 内容：The KING arrives at Rousillon and mourns HELENA as a lost jewel. COUNTESS shares the grief while the court prepares to pardon BERTRAM and arrange his new future.

## AWTEW-ACT5-SG06：Bertram Seeks Pardon

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT5-SG06
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Rousillon，COUNT's palace，formal hall.
- 人物：BERTRAM, KING, COUNTESS, LAFEU, attendants
- 内容：BERTRAM kneels and asks forgiveness for rejecting HELENA. The KING accepts his repentance and moves toward marrying him to LAFEU's daughter.

## AWTEW-ACT5-SG07：The Ring Raises Suspicion

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT5-SG07
- 时长建议：10-15s
- 场景：Rousillon，COUNT's palace，formal hall.
- 人物：KING, BERTRAM, LAFEU, COUNTESS, attendants
- 内容：LAFEU notices on BERTRAM's hand the ring the KING gave HELENA. BERTRAM's evasive explanations make the KING suspect foul play in HELENA's disappearance.

## AWTEW-ACT5-SG08：Diana Accuses Bertram

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT5-SG08
- 时长建议：10-15s
- 场景：Rousillon，COUNT's palace，formal hall.
- 人物：DIANA, Widow, BERTRAM, KING, LAFEU, COUNTESS, attendants
- 内容：DIANA enters with the Widow and claims BERTRAM seduced and promised her marriage. BERTRAM denies and insults her, while DIANA presents the ring evidence.

## AWTEW-ACT5-SG09：Parolles Confirms the Affair

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT5-SG09
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Rousillon，COUNT's palace，formal hall.
- 人物：PAROLLES, DIANA, BERTRAM, KING, LAFEU, COUNTESS
- 内容：PAROLLES is called as a witness and confirms BERTRAM's pursuit of DIANA. His testimony deepens the confusion but supports the chain of events that HELENA engineered.

## AWTEW-ACT5-SG10：Helena Reveals the Fulfilled Conditions

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT5-SG10
- 时长建议：10-15s
- 场景：Rousillon，COUNT's palace，formal hall.
- 人物：HELENA, BERTRAM, KING, COUNTESS, LAFEU, DIANA, Widow, PAROLLES, attendants
- 内容：HELENA appears alive and reveals that she has BERTRAM's ring and carries his child. The impossible conditions are fulfilled through the bed trick.

## AWTEW-ACT5-SG11：All Seems Well

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT5-SG11
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Rousillon，COUNT's palace，formal hall.
- 人物：KING, HELENA, BERTRAM, COUNTESS, LAFEU, DIANA, Widow, PAROLLES, attendants
- 内容：BERTRAM promises to love HELENA if the truth is clear. The KING orders the full story to be told, rewards DIANA, and draws the bitter past toward a reconciled ending.

## AWTEW-ACT5-SG12：Epilogue to the Audience

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT5-SG12
- 时长建议：4-6s
- 场景：Stage front after the play.
- 人物：KING
- 内容：The KING speaks the epilogue, asking the audience's patience and applause so the play may be truly well ended.
