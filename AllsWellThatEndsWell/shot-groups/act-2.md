# All's Well That Ends Well — Act II Shot Groups

Source script: ../scripts/act-2.md

## AWTEW-ACT2-SG01：Young Lords Depart for War

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT2-SG01
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Paris，KING's palace.
- 人物：KING, First Lord, Second Lord, BERTRAM, PAROLLES, attendants
- 内容：The KING sends young French lords toward the Florentine war with solemn advice. BERTRAM longs to go with them but remains restrained by the King's command.

## AWTEW-ACT2-SG02：Helena Offers the Cure

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT2-SG02
- 时长建议：10-15s
- 场景：Paris，KING's palace，sickroom or audience chamber.
- 人物：KING, HELENA, LAFEU, attendants
- 内容：LAFEU brings HELENA before the sick KING. The KING doubts her youth and her remedy, but HELENA insists her father's art and heaven's help can cure him.

## AWTEW-ACT2-SG03：The King's Wager

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT2-SG03
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Paris，KING's palace.
- 人物：KING, HELENA, LAFEU
- 内容：HELENA stakes her life on the cure and asks that, if she succeeds, she may choose a husband from the court. The KING accepts the bargain.

## AWTEW-ACT2-SG04：The Clown Sent to Court

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT2-SG04
- 时长建议：6-8s
- 场景：Rousillon，COUNT's palace.
- 人物：COUNTESS, Clown
- 内容：COUNTESS sends the Clown to court with messages. His riddling, earthy jokes turn the household errand into comic relief before the story returns to Paris.

## AWTEW-ACT2-SG05：The King's Recovery Announced

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT2-SG05
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Paris，KING's palace.
- 人物：KING, HELENA, LAFEU, PAROLLES, BERTRAM, court lords
- 内容：The court gathers after HELENA's cure succeeds. LAFEU marvels at the miracle while PAROLLES postures and BERTRAM stands among the eligible young lords.

## AWTEW-ACT2-SG06：Helena Chooses Bertram

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT2-SG06
- 时长建议：10-15s
- 场景：Paris，KING's palace，formal court.
- 人物：HELENA, BERTRAM, KING, LAFEU, court lords
- 内容：HELENA passes over other nobles and chooses BERTRAM as her husband. BERTRAM recoils at her lower birth and refuses the match in front of the court.

## AWTEW-ACT2-SG07：The Forced Marriage

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT2-SG07
- 时长建议：10-15s
- 场景：Paris，KING's palace，formal court.
- 人物：KING, BERTRAM, HELENA, LAFEU, PAROLLES, attendants
- 内容：The KING rebukes BERTRAM's pride and commands him to marry HELENA. BERTRAM submits outwardly, while LAFEU sees through PAROLLES's swagger and the court prepares the wedding.

## AWTEW-ACT2-SG08：Bertram's Flight Decided

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT2-SG08
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Paris，KING's palace，after the ceremony.
- 人物：BERTRAM, PAROLLES
- 内容：BERTRAM refuses to consummate the marriage and plans to run to the Florentine war. PAROLLES encourages the escape, framing military adventure as preferable to domestic obedience.

## AWTEW-ACT2-SG09：Helena Learns Bertram Is Leaving

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT2-SG09
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Paris，KING's palace.
- 人物：HELENA, Clown, PAROLLES
- 内容：HELENA receives news from the Clown and PAROLLES that BERTRAM intends to depart that night. Her wedding joy is immediately replaced by uncertainty and humiliation.

## AWTEW-ACT2-SG10：A Cold Farewell

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT2-SG10
- 时长建议：10-15s
- 场景：Paris，KING's palace.
- 人物：BERTRAM, HELENA, LAFEU, PAROLLES
- 内容：LAFEU warns BERTRAM against trusting PAROLLES. BERTRAM gives HELENA a brief, cold farewell, sends her back to Rousillon, and leaves for Florence instead of joining her as husband.
