# All's Well That Ends Well — Act I Shot Groups

Source script: ../scripts/act-1.md

## AWTEW-ACT1-SG01：Mourning at Rousillon

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT1-SG01
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Rousillon，COUNT's palace，mourning chamber.
- 人物：COUNTESS, BERTRAM, HELENA, LAFEU
- 内容：The household stands in black after the Count's death. COUNTESS grieves losing her son to the King's wardship; BERTRAM prepares to leave, and LAFEU speaks of the sick King of France.

## AWTEW-ACT1-SG02：Gerard de Narbon's Cure Remembered

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT1-SG02
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Rousillon，COUNT's palace，同一 mourning chamber.
- 人物：COUNTESS, LAFEU, BERTRAM, HELENA
- 内容：COUNTESS and LAFEU remember HELENA's father Gerard de Narbon, whose medical skill might have cured the King's fistula. HELENA remains quiet, absorbing the possibility.

## AWTEW-ACT1-SG03：Bertram Leaves for Court

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT1-SG03
- 时长建议：6-8s
- 场景：Rousillon，COUNT's palace exit.
- 人物：BERTRAM, COUNTESS, LAFEU, HELENA
- 内容：BERTRAM takes formal leave for the French court. COUNTESS blesses him; HELENA watches the man she loves depart beyond her reach.

## AWTEW-ACT1-SG04：Helena's Hidden Love

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT1-SG04
- 时长建议：10-15s
- 场景：Rousillon，COUNT's palace，after the others exit.
- 人物：HELENA, PAROLLES
- 内容：HELENA confesses in soliloquy that she loves BERTRAM despite the gulf in rank. PAROLLES enters with bawdy talk of virginity, turning her private longing into a sharper resolve.

## AWTEW-ACT1-SG05：Helena Forms the Paris Plan

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT1-SG05
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Rousillon，COUNT's palace，quiet interior.
- 人物：HELENA
- 内容：After PAROLLES exits, HELENA decides to go to Paris and attempt the King's cure with her father's remedies, hoping that medicine may open a path toward BERTRAM.

## AWTEW-ACT1-SG06：The War Question in Paris

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT1-SG06
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Paris，KING's palace.
- 人物：KING, First Lord, Second Lord, BERTRAM, attendants
- 内容：The sick KING receives news of the Florentine and Senoy war. He refuses direct French intervention but allows young nobles to seek honour in Italy on their own.

## AWTEW-ACT1-SG07：Bertram Presented to the King

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT1-SG07
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Paris，KING's palace.
- 人物：KING, BERTRAM, First Lord, Second Lord
- 内容：BERTRAM is presented as the late Count's son and ward. The KING remembers BERTRAM's father warmly and urges the young man to inherit his father's virtues.

## AWTEW-ACT1-SG08：A Household Rumour About Helena

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT1-SG08
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Rousillon，COUNT's palace.
- 人物：COUNTESS, Steward, Clown
- 内容：The Steward tells COUNTESS that he overheard HELENA confessing love for BERTRAM. The Clown's comedy delays the matter, but COUNTESS recognizes the seriousness beneath the report.

## AWTEW-ACT1-SG09：Countess Confronts Helena

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT1-SG09
- 时长建议：10-15s
- 场景：Rousillon，COUNT's palace，private room.
- 人物：COUNTESS, HELENA
- 内容：COUNTESS tests HELENA by calling herself her mother. HELENA breaks down, confesses that she loves BERTRAM, and fears the confession makes her unworthy.

## AWTEW-ACT1-SG10：Blessing for the Cure

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT1-SG10
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Rousillon，COUNT's palace，private room.
- 人物：COUNTESS, HELENA
- 内容：HELENA reveals her plan to heal the KING using her father's medicine. COUNTESS, moved by her courage and love, gives HELENA money, blessing, and permission to travel to Paris.
