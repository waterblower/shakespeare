# All's Well That Ends Well — Act IV Shot Groups

## AWTEW-ACT4-SG01：Ambush at the Hedge-Corner

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT4-SG01
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Outside the Florentine camp，night ambush point.
- 人物：Second Lord, First Soldier, soldiers, PAROLLES
- 内容：The lords and soldiers hide near the path where PAROLLES must pass. They prepare nonsense foreign speech so he will believe he has been captured by the enemy.

## AWTEW-ACT4-SG02：Parolles Captured

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT4-SG02
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Outside the Florentine camp，same ambush point.
- 人物：PAROLLES, Second Lord, First Soldier, soldiers
- 内容：PAROLLES enters inventing excuses about the lost drum. The disguised soldiers seize, blindfold, and terrify him into thinking his life depends on confession.

## AWTEW-ACT4-SG03：Diana Bargains with Bertram

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT4-SG03
- 时长建议：10-15s
- 场景：Florence，Widow's house.
- 人物：BERTRAM, DIANA
- 内容：BERTRAM presses DIANA with vows and seduction. DIANA resists, then sets the terms of the secret meeting: his ancestral ring in exchange for the night he seeks.

## AWTEW-ACT4-SG04：The Midnight Exchange Set

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT4-SG04
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Florence，Widow's house，private threshold.
- 人物：BERTRAM, DIANA
- 内容：DIANA instructs BERTRAM to come in darkness, speak little, and leave quickly. The conditions align him unknowingly with HELENA's plan.

## AWTEW-ACT4-SG05：News of Helena's Death

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT4-SG05
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：The Florentine camp.
- 人物：First Lord, Second Lord, BERTRAM, soldiers
- 内容：The lords discuss letters from Rousillon and the report that HELENA is dead. BERTRAM prepares to return home, believing his unwanted marriage has ended.

## AWTEW-ACT4-SG06：Parolles Betrays Everyone

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT4-SG06
- 时长建议：10-15s
- 场景：The Florentine camp，mock interrogation.
- 人物：PAROLLES, First Lord, Second Lord, BERTRAM, soldiers
- 内容：Blindfolded PAROLLES answers the false captors by slandering BERTRAM and betraying military information. BERTRAM and the lords listen as his cowardice becomes undeniable.

## AWTEW-ACT4-SG07：The Braggart Unmasked

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT4-SG07
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：The Florentine camp.
- 人物：PAROLLES, BERTRAM, First Lord, Second Lord, soldiers
- 内容：The blindfold is removed and PAROLLES sees his friends were the captors. Shamed and exposed, he abandons his heroic image and accepts survival in disgrace.

## AWTEW-ACT4-SG08：Helena Heads for the King

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT4-SG08
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Florence，Widow's house，preparing for travel.
- 人物：HELENA, Widow, DIANA
- 内容：HELENA thanks the Widow and DIANA for completing the exchange. They prepare to go to the King so the ring, the pregnancy, and DIANA's testimony can reveal the truth.

## AWTEW-ACT4-SG09：Rousillon Expects Bertram Home

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT4-SG09
- 时长建议：8-12s
- 场景：Rousillon，COUNT's palace.
- 人物：COUNTESS, LAFEU, Clown
- 内容：COUNTESS and LAFEU mourn HELENA and blame PAROLLES for misleading BERTRAM. LAFEU proposes his daughter as a new match while the household expects BERTRAM's return.

## AWTEW-ACT4-SG10：The Clown Reports Parolles's Fall

- 编号：AWTEW-ACT4-SG10
- 时长建议：6-8s
- 场景：Rousillon，COUNT's palace.
- 人物：Clown, LAFEU, COUNTESS
- 内容：The Clown brings comic news that PAROLLES has become shabby and disgraced. The palace prepares for BERTRAM's arrival under the false belief that HELENA is gone forever.
